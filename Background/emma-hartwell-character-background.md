## Character Overview

**Full name:** Emma Catherine Hartwell  
**Age:** 23, turning 24 in two weeks (born September 18, 1996 - 5 months older than Ashley)  
**Hometown:** Kenilworth, IL (wealthy North Shore suburb)  
**Education:** Loyola University Chicago, B.A. in Communications (2014-2018)  
**Previous occupation:** Marketing Coordinator (2018-2020)  
**Current occupation (as of August 2020):** Customer Success Coordinator at tech startup, fully remote  
**Relationship to Ashley:** Best friend since freshman year Loyola, roommate since 2018

**Physical description:**
- 5'6", slender build, naturally thin (doesn't have to work at it, slight source of tension when Ashley complains about weight)
- Blonde hair (highlighted, expensive salon maintenance), blue eyes
- Polished appearance - looks like money even in casual clothes
- Dresses well (designer labels when shopping with family money, J.Crew when on her own budget)
- Elegant in a way Ashley isn't - Ashley is warm and approachable, Emma is cool and composed

**Personality:**
- Witty, sharp humor, slightly dark/sarcastic edge
- Intelligent but hides it (family doesn't value female intelligence)
- Fiercely loyal once she trusts you (takes time to earn trust)
- Generous with friends, stingy with family
- Drinks more than Ashley (wine most nights, concerning but not yet problematic)
- Independent, refuses family help even when struggling
- Conflict avoidant with family, direct with friends
- Charming when needed (learned to perform for parents' world)

**Location:** Kenilworth, IL - one of the wealthiest suburbs on Chicago's North Shore

### Father: Charles Harrison Hartwell III

**Age:** 62 (born 1963)  
**Occupation:** Managing Partner at prestigious Chicago law firm (corporate litigation)  
**Income:** $800K+/year, multi-million net worth  
**Education:** Yale undergrad, Harvard Law

**Personality & Behavior:**
- Classic narcissist - charming in public, cruel in private
- Workaholic who uses work to avoid family
- Drinks heavily (expensive scotch, maintains function)

**First real conversation (September 2014):**
- Late night in dorm lounge, both avoiding homework
- Ashley talking about big family, Emma mentioned sister

- Ashley: "Families are complicated." Emma appreciated lack of judgment
