## Overview: The Worried One

Linda Marie Morrison (nÃ©e Williams), age 61, born April 22, 1964. Retired elementary school teacher (4th grade, 30 years with Naperville School District). Lives alone in Naperville family home where she raised Kate and Mike. Widowed by divorce (2001), navigating aging and loneliness while trying to stay connected to adult children who need her less than she needs them.

Where Kate is analytical and distant, Linda is emotional and reaching. Where Mike is comfortable and easy, Linda is anxious and uncertain. Linda represents anxious maternal love that both sustains and suffocates - she loves her children desperately but doesn't know how to connect with them, especially Kate. She worries about everything, wants to help with everything, understands almost nothing about who her daughter has become.

Linda is kind woman who did her best in difficult circumstances - single mother after bitter divorce, raising two kids while teaching full-time, navigating her own mother's anxiety while trying not to pass it to her children (failed at that). Now retired with too much time and too little purpose, she fills days with book club and library volunteering while wishing her children would call more, visit more, need her more.
