## Overview

Marcus Chen is Paul's closest male friend - a 37-year-old former software consultant who left tech entirely in 2020 after severe burnout, transitioned to hospitality, and now works as Assistant General Manager at The Loyalist in Chicago's West Loop. Their friendship spans over a decade, forged through shared consulting misery, and was solidified when Paul gave Marcus a place to live during his darkest period. They maintain regular contact through monthly Blackhawks games and casual hangouts, with Marcus crediting Paul with literally saving his life.

**Key Dynamic:** Marcus is the extroverted counterbalance to Paul's introversion - charming, high-energy, intensely social - but shares Paul's underlying thoughtfulness and value system. Their friendship works because Marcus never demands more than Paul can give socially, while Paul provides steady, reliable support that Marcus desperately needed during crisis.
